Career Q&A Forum

How do I become a Forensic Accountant? What's the career path like?

Forensic Accounting Finance Investigation

I'm fascinated by financial crime investigation and want to combine accounting with detective work. What educational background do I need, and what does the day-to-day work actually involve?

2 hours ago
Dr. Sanjay Mehta ✓ Verified Expert - Forensic Accountant

Great question! Forensic accounting is an exciting field that combines detective work with financial expertise. Here's the typical path:

  • Start with a Bachelor's in Accounting/Finance + CPA certification
  • Gain 2-3 years traditional accounting experience
  • Pursue CFE (Certified Fraud Examiner) certification
  • Consider Master's in Forensic Accounting or related field

Day-to-day work involves analyzing financial records, interviewing witnesses, preparing legal reports, and sometimes testifying in court. Starting salary ranges from ₹8-15 LPA, but can reach ₹50+ LPA with experience.

Food Science Product Development Innovation

Career Change Wildlife Photography Passion Career
